# DinePro 🍽️  
Free Bootstrap Restaurant Website Template

DinePro is a **free, modern, and responsive Bootstrap restaurant website template** designed for restaurants, cafés, bistros, food courts, and culinary businesses of all kinds. Built with **Bootstrap 5.3.8**, it delivers a fast, mobile-first experience across all devices.

The template is **SCSS-based**, well-documented, and developer-friendly, making customization simple and efficient. With a complete set of ready-to-use pages, you can launch a professional restaurant website in minutes—no heavy setup required.

## ✨ Features & Pages Included

- **Home Page** – Eye-catching layout to showcase your brand and specials  
- **About Us** – Share your story, vision, and values  
- **Menu Page** – Display food and drink offerings in a clean, organized format  
- **Reservation Page** – Allow customers to book tables effortlessly  
- **Our Chefs** – Introduce your culinary team with style  
- **Gallery Page** – Showcase dishes, interiors, and events  
- **Testimonials** – Build trust with customer reviews  
- **FAQ Page** – Answer common customer questions  
- **Blog Pages** – Share updates, recipes, and restaurant news  
- **Contact Page** – Simple and user-friendly contact form  
- **Privacy Policy Page** – Ready for compliance and transparency
